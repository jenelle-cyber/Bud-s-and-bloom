About Us

Bud’s and Bloom Plant Nursery is proudly Jamaican-owned and dedicated to helping families, gardeners, and farmers bring lush, fruitful life to their spaces. From sweet mango trees to zesty lime plants, we supply high-quality fruit trees and expert guidance on growing them.

Our Plants

  • Citrus Trees: Orange, Lime, Lemon
  • Fruit Trees: Mango, Strawberry, Lychee, Apple, June Plum

Plant Care Tips

  • Water plants early in the morning or late in the evening.
  • Use compost-rich soil for better growth.
  • Prune regularly to promote new buds and blooms.
  • Protect young trees from pests and excessive sun.
